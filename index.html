About me

I'm passionate about tech and art!

Based in Riga, I’m a front-end web developer and web designer whose goal is to make your ideas come to life. I care about clean and functional design, precision and accessibility. I’m always striving for the best results to achieve my own and my clients' goals!
I create websites for entrepreneurs, small businesses and anyone who wants to increase their presence online.
My background is in art - I have a Bachelor's degree in Art History and Theory and 7 years of work experience at a contemporary art gallery. Having art around me daily at my work and my personal space has developed an eye for a good design. I love a minimalistic aesthetic with little touches and accents, making every website unique.

Skills: HTML5, CSS3, PSD to HTML, Git, JavaScript, jQuery, WordPress
